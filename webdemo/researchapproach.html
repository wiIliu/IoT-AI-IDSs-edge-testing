Research Approach

The methodology followed to design, evaluate, and benchmark machine learning and deep learning-based intrusion detection models.

  • Train and evaluate ML and CNN models using the RT-IoT-2022 dataset.
  • Perform binary and multi-class intrusion classification.
  • Apply model tuning, optimization, and ensemble evaluation.
  • Measure accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, MCC, and runtime performance.
