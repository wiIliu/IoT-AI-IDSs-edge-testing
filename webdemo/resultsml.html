Results for Traditional Machine Learning Models

The goal of these models was to perform both binary intrusion detection (benign vs. malicious traffic) and multiclass attack classification. The results shown below summarize the performance of the two baseline models used for comparison: Random Forest and XGBoost.

Performance metrics such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score were calculated to assess each model’s effectiveness in identifying network threats and distinguishing between multiple attack categories.
