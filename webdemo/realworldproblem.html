Real-World Problem

Why stronger intrusion detection systems are needed in modern IoT ecosystems.

  • IoT devices are rapidly increasing in homes, healthcare, industry, and infrastructure.
  • Most IoT devices have limited processing power and cannot run strong encryption or security protocols.
  • As a result, IoT networks are vulnerable to:
    • Malware
    • Data theft
    • Unauthorized remote device control
  • Traditional cybersecurity tools struggle with scale, attack diversity, and speed requirements.
  • There is a growing demand for adaptive, intelligent, real-time detection solutions.
