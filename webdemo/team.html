Our project team consists of five dedicated researchers and developers who collaborated to design, build, and evaluate intrusion detection solutions for IoT environments.

Josh Quintano

CNN Back-end Developer

Joshua researched deep learning models and current trends in deep learning regarding the types of models used for intrusion detection in various research areas. Austin Tomo

CNN Back-end Developer

Austin developed and implemented a CNN model for multi-class classification across all 12 attack types in the dataset. He adapted the tabular data into a structure suitable for CNN input and adjusted the architecture to handle multi-class output. He also integrated a self-attention layer into the multi-class CNN architecture and fine-tuned the model to improve accuracy.
