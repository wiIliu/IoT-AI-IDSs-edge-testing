Meet the Team

Our project team consists of five dedicated researchers and developers who collaborated to design, build, and evaluate intrusion detection solutions for IoT environments.

Chelsea Pfeiffer

Project Manager & Front-end Developer

Chelsea led the team to make sure our documentation was organized and submitted on time. She also built this website that displays our final product and organizes the information we have compiled throughout the semester, along with the project requirements.

Josh Quintano

CNN Back-end Developer

Joshua researched deep learning models and the current trends in deep learning regarding the types of models used popularly for intrusion detection in various research areas. He also developed the base CNN model that performed binary classification of messages. He has continued to fine-tune the training parameters of the model and has also added a self-attention layer to the model to explore the use of self-attention in feature pattern recognition.

Austin Tomo

CNN Back-end Developer

Austin developed and implemented a CNN model for multi-class classification on all 12 attack types in the dataset. He adapted the tabular data into a structure suitable for CNN input and adjusted the architecture to handle multi-class output. He also integrated a self-attention layer into the multi-class CNN architecture to explore if its use would further improve the model’s performance. He fine-tuned the model’s parameters to improve accuracy.
