Ethical Impact

Balancing the benefits of AI-driven intrusion detection with responsible and ethical use of network data.

Positive Impacts

  • Improves IoT security and privacy by detecting malicious activity earlier.
  • Protects vulnerable systems such as healthcare, critical infrastructure, and home networks.
  • Supports responsible AI-assisted decision-making for security teams.

Ethical Risks

  • Potential model bias or false detections that could lead to incorrect alerts or blocked traffic.
  • Possible misuse of IDS data for surveillance or non-security purposes.
  • Network traffic must be collected, stored, and analyzed responsibly in compliance with privacy regulations and cybersecurity laws.
